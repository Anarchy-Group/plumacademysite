✨ About Plum Academy

Built for modern learners.

Plum Academy was created to make high-quality education more accessible, flexible, and engaging for students of all ages. From school revision to future-focused online courses, our mission is to create a smarter way to learn online.

Our Story

A new approach to online education.

Plum Academy was founded with the belief that online learning should feel modern, welcoming, and genuinely helpful. Traditional educational platforms can often feel outdated or difficult to engage with, especially for younger internet-native students.


Our goal is to combine strong educational foundations with digital-first learning experiences that feel intuitive, motivating, and accessible across every device.

Our Mission

Helping students learn with confidence.

We believe education should empower learners, not overwhelm them. Plum Academy focuses on supportive learning experiences, clear explanations, flexible access, and practical skills designed for the modern world.


Whether someone is revising for GCSEs or learning career-focused digital skills, we aim to make education more approachable and enjoyable.

The Academy Structure

Two learning pathways.

Plum Academy is organised into two specialist branches, each focused on different learner needs and educational goals.

01

Plum School

Plum School supports British primary and secondary students through revision hubs, online lessons, subject support, and exam preparation resources aligned with the UK curriculum.

02

Plum Courses

Plum Courses focuses on older teens and adult learners, offering practical digital skills, creative learning, technology education, and career-focused online courses.
