Common questions.

Plum School is designed for British primary and secondary school students seeking revision support and online learning resources.

What is Plum Courses?

Plum Courses provides modern online learning opportunities for older teens and adults, focusing on practical and digital skills.

Is learning fully online?

Yes — Plum Academy is designed as a flexible online-first learning platform accessible from anywhere.
